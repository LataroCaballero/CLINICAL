## Decisions Made

- Two exception types used deliberately: `NotFoundException` when the patient UUID doesn't exist in DB (404), `BadRequestException` when the patient exists but has no assigned professional (400 business rule violation)
- `PrismaService` was already injected as `this.prisma` — no constructor or module changes required
