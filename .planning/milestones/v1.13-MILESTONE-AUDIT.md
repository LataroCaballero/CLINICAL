## 4. Warnings (non-blocking)

**W-1 — Consent/indicaciones steps not stepper-actionable + no crm-kanban invalidation hook.**
`consentimiento` and `indicacionesPreop` are read-only sub-indicators in the stepper (no quick-action button). They complete only via the v1.12 consent portal (`ConsentimientoFirmado.firmadoAt`/`indicacionesLeidasAt`), which is out of v1.13 scope and has no `['crm-kanban']` invalidation. Consequence: for a patient whose *only* remaining pending step is consent/indicaciones, board-hide (EMBUDO-04) depends on the external portal + passive `staleTime`(30s)/refocus refetch rather than an immediate invalidation. By design per Phase 59 D-04. Affects EMBUDO-04 completeness only under that specific step combination. Consider adding a `crm-kanban` invalidation on consent/indicaciones completion in a future milestone if immediate board-hide is desired.
