## Integration Check

**18/20 connections wired correctly. 2 broken flows.**

### Broken Flow 1: Treatment snapshot not recorded without consumirInsumos=true

**Affects:** LIVHC-05, PAC-01
**Root cause:** `historia-clinica.service.ts` — the block that builds `tratamientosSnapshot` and writes `contenido.tratamientos` is nested inside `if (dto.consumirInsumos && dto.tratamientoIds?.length)`. When the user selects treatments that have no insumos (no checkbox shown), or leaves the checkbox unchecked, the snapshot is empty.

**Symptom:** Patients who receive catalog treatments without consuming stock show `—` in the Tab Tratamientos "Ultimo tratamiento" column.

**Fix location:** `backend/src/modules/historia-clinica/historia-clinica.service.ts`
Split the `if (dto.consumirInsumos)` block: move the treatment name fetch and `contenido.tratamientos` assignment to a separate `if (dto.tratamientoIds?.length)` block. Keep insumos aggregation and `OrdenConsumo.create` inside the original `consumirInsumos` guard.

---

### Broken Flow 2: FACTURADOR role sees /stock/consumo but backend returns 403

**Affects:** STOCK-03
**Root cause:** `ordenes-consumo.controller.ts` line 8: `@Auth('ADMIN', 'PROFESIONAL', 'SECRETARIA')` — FACTURADOR omitted.
Frontend `permissions.ts` grants FACTURADOR access to `/dashboard/stock/*` and Sidebar shows the sub-link.
Comparison: `ordenes-compra.controller.ts` line 17 includes FACTURADOR.

**Fix location:** `backend/src/modules/ordenes-consumo/ordenes-consumo.controller.ts`
Add `'FACTURADOR'` to `@Auth()` decorator. Verify `getProfesionalId()` handles FACTURADOR role same as SECRETARIA (via query param or JWT resolution).
