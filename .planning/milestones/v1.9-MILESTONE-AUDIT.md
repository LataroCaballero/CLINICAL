## Tech Debt — RESOLVED

**Phase 45 — FORM-04 price fallback for SECRETARIA (low severity) — ✅ FIXED**
`frontend/src/components/live-turno/tabs/hc/PrimeraConsultaForm.tsx:97` previously called
`useTratamientosProfesional()` without forwarding the `profesionalId` prop. For
SECRETARIA, `/tratamientos/me` returned 403 → fallback list empty → a TratamientoHC with
a null price FK showed `precio=0` in the presupuesto.
**Resolution:** changed to `useTratamientosProfesional(false, profesionalId ?? undefined)`,
mirroring `HCCreatorForm.tsx:86`. Type-checked clean (`tsc --noEmit`).
