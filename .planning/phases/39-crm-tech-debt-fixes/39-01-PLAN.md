<objective>
Fix two backend behavioral asymmetries from the v1.7 audit.

TD-1: `rechazar()` in presupuestos.service.ts unconditionally moves a patient to PERDIDO even when the patient is already in CONFIRMADO or PROCEDIMIENTO_REALIZADO. The sibling method `rechazarByToken()` has the correct guard — replicate it.

TD-3: `getKanban` uses `take: 1` ordered by `createdAt desc`, so a patient with an old ACEPTADO presupuesto and a newer BORRADOR will return the BORRADOR — causing `getEtapaWarning` to fire a false CONFIRMADO warning. Fix by fetching all non-RECHAZADO presupuestos and picking ACEPTADO first in the `.map()`.

Purpose: Close security gap in staff rechazar path and eliminate false-positive warnings in the kanban for multi-presupuesto patients.
Output: Two surgical edits — no new routes, no schema changes, no new dependencies.
</objective>
