## New Gaps Found

### CRITICAL — MISSING-NEW-1: `registradoPorId` never populated

**Affects:** DASH-05, LOG-01

Phase 5 Plan 01 added `ContactoLog.registradoPorId` (FK to Usuario) to enable per-coordinator attribution in `getCoordinatorPerformance()`. The schema field exists and the migration was applied. However, the service method that creates ContactoLogs was never updated to receive or write the field:

- `pacientes.controller.ts:123` — receives `req.user` (has `userId`) but calls `this.pacientesService.createContacto(pacienteId, profesionalId, dto)` — no `userId` argument
- `pacientes.service.ts createContacto()` — signature only accepts `(pacienteId, profesionalId, dto)`, writes no `registradoPorId`

**Result:** All ContactoLog rows have `registradoPorId = null`. `getCoordinatorPerformance()` groups by this field and returns a single row "Sin asignar" for all contacts. `CoordinatorPerformanceWidget` renders but shows meaningless aggregated data — no per-coordinator breakdown is possible.

**Fix:** Pass `req.user.id` (userId) through the controller → service → Prisma create.

---

### HIGH — MISSING-NEW-2: `PROCEDIMIENTO_REALIZADO` absent from funnel

**Affects:** DASH-01

`EtapaCRM.PROCEDIMIENTO_REALIZADO` was added in Phase 4 (set by `cerrarSesion()` for surgery appointments). It exists in the Kanban board and in the CRM auto-transitions, but was not added to:

- `ETAPAS_FUNNEL` constant in `crm-dashboard.service.ts:5-11`
- `CRMFunnelWidget` label map in `CRMFunnelWidget.tsx:6-12`

**Result:** Patients in `PROCEDIMIENTO_REALIZADO` stage are invisible in the funnel. Conversion rates skip the "procedure done" milestone. The funnel shows 5 of 6 real pipeline stages.

**Fix:** Add `EtapaCRM.PROCEDIMIENTO_REALIZADO` between `PRESUPUESTO_ENVIADO` and `CONFIRMADO` in both the service constant and widget label map.
