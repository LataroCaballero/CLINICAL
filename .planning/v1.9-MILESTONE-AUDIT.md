## Tech Debt (1 item — non-blocking)

**Phase 45 — FORM-04 price fallback for SECRETARIA (low severity)**
`frontend/src/components/live-turno/tabs/hc/PrimeraConsultaForm.tsx:97` calls
`useTratamientosProfesional()` without forwarding the `profesionalId` prop. For
SECRETARIA, `/tratamientos/me` returns 403 → fallback list empty → a TratamientoHC with
a null price FK shows `precio=0` in the presupuesto. Primary price path (backend join)
is unaffected and PROFESIONAL works fully.
**Fix:** `useTratamientosProfesional(false, profesionalId)` — mirrors `HCCreatorForm.tsx:86`.
